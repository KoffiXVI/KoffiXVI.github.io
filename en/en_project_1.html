Space Fighter

Presentation

Space Fighter is a game that I created by the end of 2022. It's a shoot 'em up in which you play as a spaceship fending off waves of enemies. This game is a revisited version of an old one I made with Scratch back when I was in middle school. At that time, I was inpired by Space Invaders to create it.

Development

Space Fighter was fully developped with Python, thanks to the PyGame library. It took me a month and a half to reach the final version of this game. Working on Space Fighter helped me get more familiar with Python's environment and gave me the confidence to take on more complex projects in game development.

How to play

Commands

  • ←: Move left
  • →: Move right
  • ↑: Move up
  • ↓: Move down
  • SPACEBAR: shoot on enemies
  • ENTER: pause button

To eliminate an enemy, shoot it until it's destroyed. Ramming into them is also an option, however, you'll also take some damage.

There are 4 enemies/obstacles in total. Enemy spaceships appear in higher numbers based on the Wave level.
The meteor is a particular entity that cannot be destroyed and can only be avoided.
In order to face its enemies, the player's spaceship can have access to various boosts.

Power-Ups

Power-ups are bonuses that appear randomly during waves. Collect the power-up bubble to receive a random boost.

IconNameEffect
Speed + Raises movement and shooting speed. Stacks.
Ammo 1 Changes player's ammo type. Deals 15 damage per shot.
Energy + Restore some player's life (20) and energy (5).
If a shield is active, it also receives some energy (30).
Shield A shield takes damage for the player (200 Life). Its durability can be restored.
Ammo 2 Changes player's ammo type. Deal only 10 damage per shot, but shooting speed is increased and shots are wider.
Ammo 3 Changes player's ammo type. Deals 20 damage per shot.
Battery Restore some player's life (20) and energy (5).
If a shield is active, it also receives some energy (30).

NB: Battery is a power up guaranteed to appear every 3 waves.
